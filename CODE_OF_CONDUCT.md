# Code of Conduct

This project exists to lower the temperature of an argument, so we hold ourselves to
the standard we are asking of everyone else.

**Be exact rather than heated.** The people who disagree with this standard include
people whose livelihoods are genuinely threatened, and people who genuinely believe
disclosure will be weaponised against them. Both are arguing in good faith more often
than not.

**Attack the definition, not the person.** "Level 3's boundary is wrong because X" is
the most valuable thing you can bring here. "You are shilling for AI" is not.

**No purity tests.** Contributors who use AI heavily and contributors who refuse to
touch it are equally welcome, and both are needed. A standard written only by one camp
would be trusted by only one camp.

**Harassment, personal attacks, and bad-faith brigading** get you removed, once,
without a debate about it.

Report problems by opening an issue, or privately to the maintainer at
[karauda.com/contact](https://karauda.com/contact).

Adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant.
